Denver Health

About the company

Denver Health was founded as City Hospital in 1860 to serve the health care needs of the rapidly developing city of Denver. Since then, we have grown alongside the community to become a complete health care system, proudly providing care for all residents -- at every point in their lives. We believe healthy people are the foundation of a vibrant community, and Denver Health has been treating and healing the people of Denver for more than 160 years.

Job Description

As a central supply technician at Denver Health, a level 1 trauma center that operates 24/7, I had the important responsibility of maintaining, rotating, and filling all the supplies in the hospital. It was crucial that I be detail-oriented in order to ensure that the supply rooms were stocked efficiently, so that the medical staff had access to the necessary equipment and supplies at all times. This helped to ensure that the hospital's operations ran smoothly.

Job success

At Denver Health, I was held to a high standard of work. This standard was divided into several areas. One of my responsibilities was to keep the supply rooms organized and ensure that all supply carts were easily accessible at all times. This allowed medical staff to quickly find the necessary supplies to perform their duties. Another part of the standard was to ensure that all supplies were in good condition and not expired, in order to maintain the safety of patients. In addition, if any medical staff requested a particular item, I would deliver it promptly, recognizing that time is of the essence in a healthcare setting. My dedication to meeting these standards earned me an award for my leadership and commitment, especially during a staff shortage caused by the Covid-19 pandemic.

Conclusion

I had a very positive experience while working at Denver Health. I was able to gain valuable skills and make lasting connections that will benefit me throughout my career. I am grateful for the recognition I received during my time there. I am eager to apply what I learned at Denver Health to my future career endeavors.

The Home Depot

About the company

The Home Depot is the world's largest home improvement retailer with approximately 500,000 orange-blooded associates and 2,300 stores in the U.S., Canada and Mexico. The typical store today averages 105,000 square feet of indoor retail space, interconnected with an e-commerce business that offers more than one million products for the DIY customer, professional contractors, and the industry's largest installation business for the Do-It-For-Me customer.

Job Description

As a Customer Service/Sales associate, my primary responsibility was to provide fast, friendly service to customers by actively seeking them out and helping them to identify their needs. I learned about the products we sold and used that knowledge to assist customers in making informed purchasing decisions. I was trained to greet, qualify, recommend, and close sales with customers in my department, as well as to have a basic understanding of products in adjacent departments. In addition to helping customers, I was also responsible for maintaining an organized and clean shopping environment in my assigned areas and following all safety policies and procedures to ensure the safety of myself, my colleagues, vendors, and customers.

Job success

At Home Depot, customer satisfaction was our top priority. We operated under the belief that the customer is always right, which helped us to consistently meet their needs and improve their shopping experience. In my role, I was responsible for managing the inventory in the garden section and ensuring that the store was well-organized. This required me to listen carefully to customers and understand their needs. I also learned how to adapt to different situations and people in order to better serve our customers. Overall, my time at Home Depot taught me the importance of customer service and how to effectively manage a retail space.

Working at Home Depot gave me the opportunity to learn how to truly listen to and understand the needs of customers.
